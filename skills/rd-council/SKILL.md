## Phase 1: Context Gathering

Gather the full picture before the council convenes:

1. Read `memory/MEMORY.md` — goals, active projects, priorities
2. Read the 3 most recent files in `memory/logs/` — what Rohan and Aeon have been doing
3. Read `memory/topics/` if any files exist — detailed project notes
4. WebFetch the GitHub profile README or recent commits:
   - `https://api.github.com/users/bludragon66613-sys/repos?sort=updated&per_page=5` — what repos are active
5. WebSearch 3 headlines: "AI agents 2026 latest", "crypto market this week" — pulse check on the world
6. Note today's date, day of week, and any patterns in Rohan's recent activity

Write a brief "Context Document" (3-5 bullet points) summarizing what Rohan is working on, what's stalled, and what's moving fast.

---

## Phase 2: The Council Convenes

Each council member reads the Context Document plus all previous members' outputs before speaking. Write each member's output to a temp section in memory, clearly labeled.

### Council Member 1 — THE ANALYST
**Role:** Systems thinker. Finds inefficiencies, underused assets, and hidden bottlenecks.
**Focus questions:**
- What is Rohan spending time on that has low leverage?
- What tools or systems are underutilized?
- What's the biggest bottleneck right now across his projects?
- What patterns appear in the last 30 days of activity?

**Output:** 3-5 specific findings, each with: *Observation → Implication → Opportunity*

---

### Council Member 2 — THE FUTURIST
**Role:** Trend spotter. Identifies where AI, crypto, and dev tools are heading and how Rohan can get ahead.
**Focus questions:**
- What's a capability that exists today that Rohan isn't using but should be?
- What will matter in 6 months that nobody is talking about now?
- Where does NERV/Aeon fit into the emerging agentic economy?
- What investment or business opportunity is opening up right now?

**Output:** 3 "bets" — each with: *Signal → Why now → How Rohan can act on it*

---

### Council Member 3 — THE DEVIL'S ADVOCATE
**Role:** Critical challenger. Stress-tests every assumption made by The Analyst and Futurist.
**Focus questions:**
- What are The Analyst and Futurist wrong about?
- What risks are being ignored?
- Which of these ideas will actually waste Rohan's time?
- What's the biggest thing that could go wrong in the next 30 days?

**Output:** 3 challenges to the most compelling ideas from Members 1-2, each with: *Claim → Counterpoint → What to watch for*

---

### Council Member 4 — THE BUILDER
**Role:** Pragmatic engineer. Turns the best surviving ideas into concrete next steps.
**Instructions:** Review what survived The Devil's Advocate's scrutiny. Pick the 2 strongest opportunities and design an implementation path for each.

**Output per idea:**
- What it is (1 sentence)
- Why it's worth building now
- The first 3 concrete steps
- Est. time to first value: hours / days / weeks
- What Aeon skill could help automate or support it

---

### Council Member 5 — THE SYNTHESIZER
**Role:** Executive advisor. Reads all four members' outputs and writes the final memo.
**Instructions:** You have heard all perspectives. Now synthesize them into a clean, actionable executive memo. Be direct. No fluff. Rohan is a solo operator with limited time — every recommendation must be worth his attention.
