## Brand Facts
- **Project:** Virāma (विराम) — 12 private villas, Rs 13–20 Cr, first residential project by SSquare
- **Location:** Prashashan Nagar, Jubilee Hills (branded as "Malibu")
- **Developer:** SSquare — est. 1991, 35 years, 10M+ sqft commercial real estate, premium builders, India
- **Developer tagline:** "The Standard, Privately Held."
- **Project tagline:** "Come Home to Quiet."
- **Logomark:** S² monogram
- **Developer palette:** Obsidian #1A1A18 / Raw Ivory #F5F0E8 / Platinum #E8E4DC / Slate #8A8A8A
- **Project palette:** Virāma Stone #2C2825 / Pavilion White / Arcadian Gold #C9A96E / Sandstone Pale / The Green Circuit #3D4A3A
- **Brand tone:** Serene opulence — minimal in expression, maximal in experience. Hushed confidence.
- **No founders named** — brand is generic to SSquare as a company

User confirmed brand should be generic to SSquare company (not attributed to founders personally).
SSquare has 35-year commercial RE track record; Virāma is their residential debut.
