- Sell outcomes not agents ("5 AI employees, INR 5K/month")

- Unit economics break at micro pricing without self-serve automation
- WhatsApp-first delivery is the actual moat (large SaaS can't replicate)
- Case study from Client #1 is highest-leverage action
